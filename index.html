Our Hosts

Samiran Ghosh

In a career spanning 27 years, Samiran has held leadership roles at McKinsey, Microsoft, IBM, Dun & Bradstreet, IBM, and TCS. In addition, he is a recognized thought leader in the AI/Blockchain space, a TEDx speaker, on the first AI & Ethics Journal editorial board, and a member of the Forbes Tech Council and World Economic Forum. Currently also a Senior Advisor/Board Member with several startups in the fintech, artificial intelligence, and blockchain space. He has also co-authored a couple of books listed amongst the top 20 new fintech books to read in 2020.

Nilesh Patankar

For over two decades, Nilesh has been involved in every aspect of developing & releasing commercial software products. He has been behind the launch of new products like mobile POS - payPLUS & replatforming projects like India’s largest coalition loyalty network - PAYBACK. 4 years of research & 11 international patents provide Archethic with the technological attributes that the predecessors have lacked - scalability, speed, reliability, & simplicity of native biometric recognition. Archethic blockchain has been designed for mass adoption.

Sheetal Choksi

For over two decades, Sheetal has spent most of her time in research & marketing & developed the unique capability of managing large projects. A seasoned strategist & communications professional, with over 25 years’ experience in research, advertising, retail & branding. Her key strength is her ability to lead & drive projects from the strategic phase right through to execution with an eye for detail. She is co-founder of two companies, Word Hatter, a strategic content company & Unpac Research, a boutique research company.
