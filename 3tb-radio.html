3 Techies Banter Goes Live : We're Launching Our Internet Radio Station!

We are buzzing with excitement to announce that 3 Techies Banter is launching its very own internet radio station! After countless episodes dissecting the latest in tech, we're taking a delightful step sideways—or perhaps backwards—into one of humanity's most enduring technologies: radio. Why Radio Might Outlive Us All Here is a thought experiment we love: When aliens arrive or the mushroom clouds bloom, what communication technology will still be standing? The answer - Radio. While our smartphones become expensive paperweights and satellite networks go dark, someone, somewhere, will be cranking up a hand-powered radio transmitter, sending Morse code or voice into the electromagnetic void. Radio waves require only a basic transmitter and receiver for their transmission. They don't require login credentials, software updates, or a stable internet connection. They just... work. Radio is the cockroach of communication technologies—practically indestructible and frustratingly simple. And we mean that as the highest compliment.
